Rishiraj is a Machine Learning Engineer and Competitions Master on Kaggle. He has worked as a Data Scientist at Celebal Technologies and specializes in the application of Deep Learning in Production. Rishiraj started his journey in tech after getting into university and soon explored almost everything: from App development to Web and from coffee to cookies. Failed in some, lost interest in some but finally, he found his love: Machine Learning and chicken wings.

Rishiraj has been a Microsoft Student Ambassador, a DeepLearning.AI Ambassador, a ML team lead at Google DSC and an active volunteer for Google Developer Groups Kolkata. He loves taking sessions and speaking at various conferences for promoting student knowledge on developer products and platforms. He also taught Python to students during the pandemic as a facilitator at Stanford University.

These are some things that you can find about him on the internet, want a secret? Rishiraj is a national level Chess player, a swimming champion and he can lecture for hours on the outer reaches of space and the craziness of astrophysics.
