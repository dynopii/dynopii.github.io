Snehangshu is an Electronics and Communication Engineer with an edge in Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Communication Systems.

In college, he actively participated in autonomous robotics and has a good knowledge of DIY electronics. He was a finalist in Smart India Hackathon 2019 Software Edition and TCS Inframind 2019 in the DevOps category. He was also a part of IEEE Electron Devices Society and helped organize many events and took sessions on electronics there.

He also published his collaborative work named “Feature Extraction and Classification of Phonocardiograms using Convolutional Neural Networks” on “IEEE International Conference for Convergence in Engineering (ICCE)” which uses machine learning on heart sound data and predict heart failure risks.

He has more than one year of experience working with Spring Boot Microservices and AWS Cloud with TCS.

Also, he is a former Linux distro hopper, PC hardware enthusiast, and photographer.
